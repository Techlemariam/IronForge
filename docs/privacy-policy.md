# Privacy Policy for IronForge

**Last Updated: 2025-12-26**

## 1. Introduction
IronForge ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application and related services.

## 2. Data Collection and Usage

### 2.1 Local Storage (Device Only)
IronForge is designed with a "Privacy-First" appproach. 
- **Workout Data**: Your logs, history, and stats are stored locally on your device or in your personal cloud database (if configured).
- **Game State**: Your character progression, inventory, and currency are stored locally.

### 2.2 Optional Integrations
You may choose to connect third-party services. If you do, we interact with their APIs as follows:
- **Hevy**: Read-only access to import your workout history. We store a local copy for gamification.
- **Strava**: Read-only access to import cardio sessions.
- **Intervals.icu**: Read-only access to wellness and training load data.
- **Google Authenticator**: Used solely for secure sign-in (if enabled via Supabase).

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

## 3. Account Deletion
You may delete your account and all associated local data at any time from within the app settings.

## 4. Children's Privacy
IronForge is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository or support channel.
